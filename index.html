Cerauno is a polished, user-friendly magazine theme with plenty of customizable features. Add secondary content in up to five widget areas, brand your content with a Site Logo or Custom Header, include links to your favorite social networks, and add Featured Images to grab your readers’ attention. Cerauno is responsive, stretching or shrinking to accommodate any screen size.

Supported Social Networks

Linking to your profile with any of the following services in the Social Links Menu will automatically display its icon:

  • CodePen
  • Digg
  • Dribbble
  • Dropbox
  • Facebook
  • Flickr
  • GitHub
  • Google+
  • Instagram
  • LinkedIn
  • Email (mailto: links)
  • Pinterest
  • Reddit
  • RSS Feed (urls with /feed/)
  • StumbleUpon
  • Tumblr
  • Twitter
  • Vimeo
  • WordPress
  • YouTube

Other Features

In addition, Cerauno supports the following features:

  • Featured Images
  • Site Logo
  • Social Links Menu
  • Custom Menus
  • Widgets
  • Custom Header
  • Custom Background
  • No Sidebars Page Template

Quick Specs (all measurements in pixels)

  1. The main column width depends on the width of the browser window, and will grow or shrink accordingly.
  2. The sidebar widths also depend on the width of the browser window.
  3. The footer widget area widths vary depending on the number of active columns and the width of the browser window.
  4. The site logo has a maximum width of 1000 and maximum height of 300.
